Instructions

  1. This is a web version of the game Simon® Says.
  2. You have 4 different coloured buttons.
  3. The computer will click a sequence of buttons after you press the start button.
  4. To play, you have to click the same sequence of buttons correctly.
  5. If you play a wrong sequence, you will have to play again.
  6. If strict mode is enabled, the counter will reset if you play a wrong sequence.
  7. After the count reaches 20, you win the game.
  8. Good Luck !!
